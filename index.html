What is hygge..?

Hygge(pronounced "hoo-gah"), is a Danish word which describes a quality of cosiness (feeling warm, comfortable, and safe). If you research 'hygge', you might come across a "Slow Living" concept, both refer to paying attention to quality of life, slowing down, and enjoying every moment.
